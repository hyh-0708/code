HUNT'N'GATHER
Apple Vision Pro is Apple’s first mixed reality (MR) headset, combining augmented reality (AR) and virtual reality (VR) technologies. Unlike traditional VR headsets, Vision Pro allows users to fluidly transition between physical and digital spaces, enabling a new paradigm Apple calls "spatial computing." With Vision Pro, users can navigate and interact with digital content using only their eyes, hands, and voice—no need for traditional controllers or remotes.
Interaction: Navigate, control, and interact with content through eye tracking, gesture recognition, and voice commands without the need for a traditional mouse or controller.
As a designer, this project made me realize that future creative work will require more than just visual or UX skills. Understanding spatial perception, behavioral interaction, and environmental integration will be just as important. It's a wake-up call for designers to think beyond screens and into three-dimensional, human-centered experiences.
